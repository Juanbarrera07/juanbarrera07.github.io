01 — About
About

Bridging spatial engineering and high-precision GeoAI for the resource sector.

I'm a Geographic & Environmental Engineer with a decade of experience across Colombia, Chile and Australia — specialised in GIS automation, drone mapping, environmental analysis and spatial product development.

My professional background spans complex geodatabase management at scale — 80+ datasets, 5–10 GB of geospatial data, 50+ cartographic deliverables for environmental licensing in the oil and mining sectors — through to independent RPAS surveying and GeoAI product development in Western Australia.

Today I bridge the gap between traditional engineering workflows and automated spatial intelligence, designing Python pipelines and applying GeoAI to turn raw drone and satellite data into operational insights for mining and environmental clients.

Through TerraForge Satellite Intelligence, I build the kind of tools I always needed in the field: desktop analytics purpose-built for mining pit characterisation, stockpile measurement, and terrain change detection.

Landslide Monitoring & Terrain Deformation Analysis

Research Project · Quebradanegra, Cundinamarca

Multi-temporal drone photogrammetry for landslide dynamics — DSM comparisons to analyse terrain deformation, hydrological influence, displacement direction and magnitude over time. Located in a complex geomorphological region of the Colombian Andes.

RESEARCH WORKFLOWRPAS Flights → Image Acquisition → Photogrammetry → DSM Generation → Multi-temporal Analysis → Deformation Mapping
DJI Phantom 4 Pro V2 Pix4D Agisoft Metashape ArcMap Multi-temporal DSM

Sub-models Built

  • Heights Sub-model: elevation and DSM change analysis
  • Water Bodies Sub-model: hydrological influence mapping
  • Vegetation change between acquisition dates
  • Direction–Time Sub-model: displacement direction over time
  • Distance–Time Sub-model: movement magnitude per period

Methodology

  • Multiple RPAS campaigns with DJI Phantom 4 Pro V2
  • Parallel processing in Pix4D and Agisoft Metashape
  • Co-registration of multi-date DSMs for change detection
  • Slope change and displacement vector analysis in ArcMap
  • Complex geomorphological terrain in Cundinamarca, Colombia
